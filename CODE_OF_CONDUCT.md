# Code of Conduct

## Our standard

Project participants are expected to communicate respectfully, focus criticism on technical work, welcome differing levels of experience, and avoid harassment or disclosure of private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

- personal attacks, threats, harassment, or discriminatory language;
- publishing another person’s private information;
- uploading private Codex conversations, credentials, or machine data without permission;
- deliberately disrupting project discussions or development.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is harmful to the project or its community.

Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact method listed on their GitHub profile.
